💜 About

Built for humans
who take breaks.

Somewhere between your third consecutive video call and your fifth coffee refill, someone had a simple idea: what if your status could just handle itself for a bit?

Presence Buddy started as a weekend experiment — a tiny browser script that prevented the dreaded yellow dot of shame from appearing while you were in the kitchen, in the garden, or just staring at the wall trying to remember what day it was.

A few iterations later, it evolved into this: a lovable little purple creature who takes the job seriously so you don't have to. No tracking, no accounts, no data — just a clean browser tool that runs entirely on your machine and disappears the moment you close the tab.

Remote work is already complicated enough. Your presence indicator shouldn't add to that anxiety. Buddy is here to take that one small stressor off your plate — permanently.
